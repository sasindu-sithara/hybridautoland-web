Our shop

Obsessed with doing hybrid right

Hybrid Drive Auto started as a two-bay experiment and grew into a dedicated service center for electrified platforms — without losing the personal touch of a neighborhood garage.

Why we chose this path

Early hybrids were misunderstood — lights on the dash, dealer quotes that didn’t match symptoms, and plenty of unnecessary pack replacements. We set out to bring engineering rigor and plain-language explanations to every repair order.

Today our team blends dealership alumni and independent specialists who actually enjoy reading TSBs and scope captures on a Friday night.

Milestones & training

A quick look at how we built depth in high-voltage service.

2009 — First HV bay

Added insulated tooling and formal lockout protocol before servicing our first Gen-II Prius battery reconditioning.

2014 — ASE L3 & OEM scan expansion

Invested in factory-level interfaces and sent lead techs for advanced electric drive certification tracks.

2019 — Plug-in surge

Expanded charger diagnostics and thermal management as PHEVs became a third of our intake mix.

Today — Community partner

Hosting seasonal “know your hybrid” clinics and partnering with local fleets on uptime planning.
